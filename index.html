Where to stay?

Guests can call 1-800-53-SUGAR and mention the Spencer - Turner Wedding to inquire about available units and discounts

Clay Brook Hotel

From hotel style rooms up to five bedroom condos, the luxury accommodations of Clay Brook are the finest the resort has to offer. Each unit is tastefully appointed down to the smallest detail. Full valet and concierge service is at your beck and call. Outdoor heated pool and hot tubs for year round enjoyment. Timbers Restaurant offers up three meals and daily. The closest to the slopes you can get.

1 Bedrooms to 4 Bedrooms from $200 to $489

Club Sugarbush

Townhouse style units, with direct outdoor access. These units offer one, two or three bedrooms with full kitchens, a living and dining area. Many have a sleeper sofa, allowing for four to eight guests per unit. Located next to the Sugarbush Inn.

1 Bedrooms to 3 Bedrooms from $149 to $267

Sugarbush Inn

A full service hotel, with the style of a traditional Vermont Country inn. Relax by the fireplace in our living room or enjoy a book in our cozy library. Continental breakfast included daily. Minimum of 30 rooms with two night stay required.

Double Occupancy for $150

Glades and Paradise

Glades and Paradise one and two bedroom condominiums are located a short shuttle ride to the lifts. The Sugarbush Village and Sugarbush Health and Racquet Club are located within walking distance.
